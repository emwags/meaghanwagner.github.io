The trades

Ghostwriting

Got an idea rattling around in your head that needs to become a book, essay, or something else entirely? That's where ghostwriting comes in—finding the structure and language that makes your vision real and gets it onto the page.

Editing

Developmental editing is about clarifying what you're actually trying to say. The work happens at the structural level—narrative arc, argument flow, whether the pieces fit together in a way that serves the whole. Sometimes it's more excavation than correction.

Building moonpaws

What if you could understand your dog through an astrological lens? That's moonpaws—a pet astrology app and content platform built with a lot of heart (and a healthy dose of whimsy). Co-running this project means writing newsletters, creating astrological guidance for dog parents, and generally making the internet a more delightful place for people who love their pups. Download the app if you're curious what the stars say about your furry friend.

Coaching

Transitions, overwhelm, the question of what comes next—coaching is for when you need clarity in the mess. As an ACC-certified coach, the approach here is less about goal-setting checklists and more about figuring out what's actually true for you right now.

Creative writing

"Dana's Inferno" is a retelling of Dante's Inferno from women's perspectives—the kind of project that simmers on the back burner until it demands attention. There's also a Substack archive on astrology and being human, currently on hiatus while it waits for its next direction.
