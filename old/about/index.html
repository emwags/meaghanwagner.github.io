Hey, I’m Meaghan Wagner (you might have guessed)…

I specialize in helping amazing people make time to focus their talents where they are most sorely needed — on themselves.

Over the last decade — yes I am old enough that it’s been a decade — I’ve helped bring organization, strategy and joy to teams and organizations throughout NYC. And in that time, I’ve encountered countless brilliant, talented, passionate people who’ve lamented that if only they could be organized the way I was, their lives would be better. Or who have insisted they just needed to find the kind of motivation I had to get where they wanted to be. To these folks, I had some magic that they felt they needed to be happier. To be healthier. To be whole.

That whole time I had a secret: It’s not magic at all. It’s simply time. Well, time and attention, which I find often go hand in hand.

That’s right, the only thing I’ve been doing differently than these wonderful people is making regular time to visualize and define where I want to go before I try to go there. That’s it. That’s the secret sauce. Don’t get me wrong, I have a lot of fun taking that time (there is color coding involved), and over the years, I’ve gotten very good at it (#humblebrag), but essentially that’s all it takes.

The problem for most people is finding that time — and making it count. Sure if you had hours every morning and weekend you could map out your future from scratch. But one thing I know about most amazing people, they don’t have that kind of time. There are usually a lot of folks counting on them every day — kids, coworkers, spouses, teams and more — making time for themselves a bit scarce.

That’s where I come in. In 30 to 60 minutes a week, we can make your talents work for you. It doesn’t have to be a lot. It just has to be. Don’t believe me? Schedule your consultation now. It’s completely free, and who knows, it might even be a little fun.
